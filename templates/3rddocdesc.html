Overview

With 23 clinics on the East Coast, OUTREACH provides quality outpatient, medically assisted treatment, and other addiction and mental health services to equip clients with the tools they need to heal.

  • Addiction Treatment Programs - Our addiction treatment programs are designed to break the cycle of addiction. We offer Vivitrol treatment, Suboxone treatment, as well as medication-assisted treatment.
  • Substance Abuse Treatment - We provide compassionate substance abuse treatment for patients healing from substances like alcohol, heroin, meth, opioids, benzos, cocaine, and fentanyl.
  • Addiction Therapy Programs - Therapy programs such as person-person therapy, nutrition education, dual diagnosis, cognitive-behavioral therapy, dialectical behavior therapy, and trauma therapy can help the whole person heal from addiction.
